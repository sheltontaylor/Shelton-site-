The Artist

Shelton

"Music is healing. Art is power. LA is home."

Shelton is a Los Angeles-based artist making music at the intersection of dystopian pop, R&B, and raw human emotion. Drawing from the streets, the shadows, and the strange beauty of the city, his sound is cinematic, vulnerable, and alive.

The Fallen Angel era is a summer-long commitment — debut music, free live shows, food drives, and a rolling bus turning LA landmarks into stages for community and connection.
